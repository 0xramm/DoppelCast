How it works

Three steps between you and a recorded clip.

No firmware, no capture drivers, no second PC. If your phone can talk to ADB, it can talk to DoppelCast.

01 / CONNECT

Plug in your phone

Connect your Android device to your PC over USB and enable USB debugging when prompted. DoppelCast detects it automatically.

02 / CONFIGURE

Set your quality

Choose resolution, frame rate, and bitrate independently — from a lightweight 1080p30 clip to a full-bitrate native-res capture.

03 / RECORD

Hit record

Capture lands on your PC as a ready-to-edit file. Stop whenever you want — no proprietary container, no re-encode required.

Why not a capture card

The hardware box was never the interesting part.

A capture card just moves pixels from one cable to another. DoppelCast reads the same signal straight off the device.

Features

Every dial a capture card locks down, unlocked.

Any resolution

From a data-light 480p up to your phone's full native display resolution.

Any frame rate

30, 60, 90, or 120fps — whatever your device's panel and game can actually push.

Any bitrate

Dial from a lightweight 5 Mbps up to broadcast-grade 100+ Mbps VBR or CBR.

H.264 & H.265

Pick the codec that matches your editor, your upload target, or your storage budget.

Synced device audio

Pull audio straight from the device in sync with video — no separate mic routing.

Built on ADB, not drivers

Uses the USB debugging bridge already on your phone. Nothing new to install on Android.
